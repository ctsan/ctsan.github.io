Trading Bubbles (A Trading Game)

26 July 2025·190 words·1 min
Author
Christos Tsanikidis, PhD
A little bit about you

Trading Bubbles is a simple game that helps you understand basic notions of trading! Do you have an eye for inefficiencies? Can you hedge your portfolio? Bet on whether randomly moving particles hit bubbles. Click once to Buy, twice to Short Sell, and a third time to reset.

For each bubble position, the market price is the % value indicated within the bubble. Read more here.

Detailed Instructions

Controls:

  • Click once on a bubble to Buy (go long).
  • Click twice to Sell (go short).
  • Click a third time to reset your bet.

Bubble Types:

  • Barrier: These bubbles pop when a particle hits them, stopping the particle in its tracks.
  • Digital: These bubbles pay out if a particle is inside them at the end of the round.
  • Knock-In: These bubbles are activated when a particle passes through them.
